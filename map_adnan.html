Adnan Syed's Day

Adnan’s day is the least specific accounting of events from the 13th - depending on what you believe, this can be either damning or liberating. If you believe he did kill Hae, then you could argue Adnan is just acting like he can’t remember anything from that day, If you believe he didn’t kill Hae, you could argue that of course he doesn’t remember anything - he wasn’t asked until weeks later and didn’t realize he needed to account for his time until the point he was arrested.

I altered the fastest route from Woodlawn to Kristi’s to incorporate the possibility they stopped somewhere to eat after track practice when Adnan was able to break fast. I decide to have the route go by the McDonald’s on rolling road since it is mentioned as a stop in Jay’s stories and occurs at a similar time.
